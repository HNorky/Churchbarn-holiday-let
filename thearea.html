Set in the beautiful, rolling Angus countryside just south of the county town of Forfar, Pitreuchie Cottage is a comfortable, spacious getaway for up to 4 adults in 3 bedrooms. The cottage is ideally located for exploring the Angus Glens, the coast and some of Scotland's internationally famous golf courses including Carnoustie, St Andrews and Murcar Links. Whether you're looking for a golfing break, hillwalking holiday or a coastal escape, Pitreuchie Cottage is the perfect base for your holiday in Scotland. The cottage has been recently renovated to a high standard, with a modern kitchen, two double bedrooms (one with ensuite), a single en-suite bedroom and a family bathroom. The large living room has ample seating, TV/FreeSat/DVD and a dining table.
